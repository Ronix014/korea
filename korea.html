Explore Seoul

Seoul is a dynamic city where modern skyscrapers meet ancient palaces, offering a unique blend of tradition and innovation.

Top Attractions

Gyeongbokgung Palace

The largest and most iconic of Seoul’s five grand palaces, showcasing Korea’s royal heritage.

N Seoul Tower

A famous landmark offering panoramic views of the city from the top of Namsan Mountain.

Bukchon Hanok Village

A charming neighborhood filled with traditional Korean houses, providing a glimpse into the past.

Myeongdong Shopping Street

A bustling shopping district known for its trendy fashion, skincare, and street food.

Lotte World Tower

One of the tallest buildings in the world, offering shopping, entertainment, and stunning city views.
